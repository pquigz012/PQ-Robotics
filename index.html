About Us

Engineered from the ground up.
Flown from experience.

PQ Robotics isn't a hobby shop. It's the product of a formal engineering education, hands-on development across military and commercial platforms, and a genuine obsession with what unmanned systems can do when they're built and maintained right.

Paul Quigley
Founder & Lead Engineer
Indiana University — B.S. Intelligent Systems Engineering

Paul's background is in intelligent systems, the intersection of hardware, autonomy, and software integration that most drone operators never get close to. At IU he specialized in the design and engineering of unmanned systems, which led directly to work developing custom drone platforms for military applications, where tolerances are tight, failure isn't an option, and off-the-shelf solutions rarely cut it.

He has built autonomous and manually-piloted aircraft from scratch, from agile FPV platforms to multi-rotor hexacopters, and handled the full engineering stack: airframe design, flight controller configuration, sensor integration, firmware troubleshooting, and cross-system compatibility. When something isn't performing, Paul doesn't guess. He diagnoses.

PQ Robotics brings that same rigor to every job, whether it's a DJI diagnostic, a custom build spec, or a cinematic shoot. This is precision work. Not toy repair.
